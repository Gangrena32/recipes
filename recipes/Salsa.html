Mango salsa

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 mangos - peeled, seeded, and diced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 clove clove of garlic, minced, or to taste
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon orange juice

Direction

Stir the mangos, green onions, garlic, cilantro, lime juice, and orange juice together in a bowl. Chill at least 1 hour before serving.

Avocado salsa

Description

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces fresh tomatillos - husked, rinsed, and halved
  • 1 large ripe avocado - halved, seeded, and flesh scooped out of the peel
  • 1/4 cup packed cilantro leaves
  • 1/2 lime, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon thinly sliced serrano chile pepper, or to taste
  • salt to taste

Direction

Place tomatillos into a food processor. Add avocado, cilantro, lime juice, serrano pepper, and salt. Process until smooth. Taste and adjust seasoning, then transfer to a serving bowl.
